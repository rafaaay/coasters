Welcome to Coasters.ph

We are Coaster Incorporated, and we offer you a new, cheaper, more efficient and a higher quality way of doing marketing. Potential customers spend lots of hours a week in bars, hotels, restaurants and events, but aren’t much confronted by good coaster marketing. Customers worldwide spend more than 10 seconds inside an establishment looking and viewing at a coaster. Besides the endless wet tables confront your customers with your marketing in their face!

Who We Are

We offer companies a unique advertising concept. Our concept is affordable and will reach your target market more efficiently than other forms of marketing. This is a new marketing concept that has proved itself very valuable in Europe. We have now brought it to Asia! Our coasters are produced from recycled paper, compressed with state of the art machinery and your marketing will be pressed onto our high quality ecofriendly German produced coasters. This eco-friendly way of cheaper and more efficient marketing will also stop the enormous waste of paper tissues in this country.

Our founders Alex Uggla, Jeroen van Straten and Joris Michl have many years of experience of doing business inside the Philippines and running marketing campaigns and business development.

What We Do

As Coasters Inc we have relationships with bars, events, hotels, restaurants, clubs and casinos as well as the companies that are willing to advertise in these establishments or companies. We also service weddings and corporate giveaways.

When it comes to running marketing campaigns this means one side of the coaster will be the establishments logo and on the other side your marketing campaign. Our signature word ‘flip me’ in the corner proofs a psychologically irresistible success towards your potential customers. When visiting an establishment an 86% rate of people play with the coasters. For the small amount of people that do not pick up the coaster they will still see your advertisement due to all the flipped coasters inside the establishment.

We ensure that we only supply you with the exact number of coasters that you will be needing for your marketing campaigns making it affordable and efficient. This is achieved by completing studies on how many visitors each establishment has per month. We will ensure that you have the right number of coasters to make the campaign run smoothly and effectively.
